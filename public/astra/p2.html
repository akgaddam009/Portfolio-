What this workflow does
When a Software / SaaS contract valued over $50,000 is submitted, Astra will automatically route it to the Legal team for first review, then to Priya Mehta (VP Procurement), and finally to Rohit Sharma (CFO) for sign-off. Each step must be completed in order. Approvers are notified by email. A reminder fires after 48 hours of inaction.
Name this workflow
Choose a name that's clear to all admins who manage workflows. This name appears in the workflow list and in approval notifications.
Activate workflow
↳ Activating makes this workflow live immediately. All matching contracts submitted from this point will follow this chain.
